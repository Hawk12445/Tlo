About

This is a simple AI Chatbot made with HTML, CSS & JavaScript. AI integrations were made possible through the Google Gemini Free API. The number of prompts is, however, limited to 1000 per day, so for better results please use precise and concise prompts. This is a demo project as future improvements will be done progressively.
